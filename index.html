RawSha | Rhythm & Rhymes

Producer · Rapper · Genre Bender

Hailing from Vadakara, Kerala, RawSha’s musical journey began in his childhood with a passion for mastering multiple instruments.
From experimenting with beats to laying down rap tracks, and fusing genres with a fearless creativity,
he’s shaping a bold, authentic sound that reverberates across the hip-hop scene in India.
Driven by rhythm and raw expression, RawSha continues to push boundaries, crafting music that’s as versatile as it is powerful.
