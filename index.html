RawSha - Discography

Producer · Rapper · Genre Bender

Hailing from Vadakara, Kerala, RawSha started stirring the musical pot as a kid, cooking up melodies across multiple instruments.
From dropping sizzling beats to spitting fire rap bars, and blending genres with fearless flair, he’s whipping up a bold, raw sound that’s making waves across India’s hip-hop scene.
Fueled by rhythm and untamed creativity, RawSha keeps turning up the heat, crafting tracks that hit hard, flow smooth, and leaves a lasting vibe.
