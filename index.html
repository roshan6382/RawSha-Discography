RawSha

Hailing from Vadakara, Kerala, RawSha started his musical journey as a producer, experimenting with beats and sounds from a young age. From mastering multiple instruments as a kid to stepping into the world of hip-hop, he has grown into a versatile artist, blending rap, production, and genre-bending creativity. His tracks carry the energy of Southside streets, raw stories, and vibes that hit deep. With over 60,000 streams, RawSha continues to push boundaries, bringing music that’s unapologetically real and infectious. Each release, from KadhaKali to Thiripp and Kedann Pedayalle, showcases his evolution and passion for crafting unique soundscapes.
